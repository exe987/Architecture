ARCHITECTURE

Architectural design is about three things: people, space and light. Our skill is to understand each in order to create beautiful spaces, both for our private clients and for commercial property developers. We carefully manage every architectural design project from defining a brief, to design, construction and the eventual handover of a completed building.

DEVELOPER & INVESTOR SERVICES

Our commercial architecture and interior design team work with developers and investors that want to achieve a financial return from their property businesses. We have deep expertise in designing for property developers and private clients too and believe this enables us to design housing solutions that are commercially successful. We work at all stages of the development cycle, from site appraisal to working with your agents to market your development.

INTERIOR DESIGN

Our interior design team works seamlessly with our Architects to deliver a luxury interior design service from concept to completion. With a strong contemporary style, we create fusions between clean architectural lines, textures and soft furnishings with a big emphasis on elegant, exciting materials that add depth and detail to a property. Our interior design team ethos is to design beautiful, yet comfortable spaces.
